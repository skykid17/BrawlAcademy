What is Brawl Stars?


It's an multiplayer online role-playing game (MORPG for short). Players of size 6 and 10 are pitted in an arena to fight against each other in groups of 3 and 2 respectively. There are multiple game modes for players to choose: duo showdown, gem grab, brawl ball and many more! Players can also team up with friends and battle other opponents. If you do not enjoy the maps available in the various game modes, no worries! Cause you can also play your very own customized maps!
